Instructions

To display a video source, press the "DISPLAY" tab at the top of the page, select the desired display (if more than one) then press the source to display.
To blank the screen, press "Blank". Select a source to unblank.
To control a device, press the "DEVICE CONTROL" tab at the top of the page. Press the icon of the device to be controlled. Press the control buttons as needed.
To select an audio source, press the "AUDIO CONTROL" tab at the top[ of the page. Press the icon of the desirec audio source. The audio source automatically follows the last-pressed video source under the "DISPLAY" tab.
To adjust microphone volume (if available), press the "MICROPHONE CONTROL" tab at the top of the page. Adjust levels as needed.
